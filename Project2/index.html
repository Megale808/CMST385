Project 2 - Final Project Proposal

1. Project Concept

Hazy Brewing is a comprehensive website designed for beer enthusiasts and homebrewers, offering a rich array of resources to inspire and guide the brewing process. The site features a detailed recipe library catering to all skill levels, with searchable filters for beer styles, ingredients, and brewing methods. A regularly updated blog delves into brewing techniques, trends, and expert tips while spotlighting the vibrant culture of craft beer. Users can explore step-by-step video tutorials on brewing basics, advanced methods, and equipment care, with live Q&A sessions to connect with professionals. A contact section allows users to submit inquiries, while community-building features, such as forums and social media integration, provide spaces for brewers to share experiences and troubleshoot challenges. With a sleek, user-friendly design optimized for all devices, BrewMaster's Corner aims to be the ultimate hub for brewing knowledge, creativity, and community, elevating the art of homebrewing.

2. Site Map

  1. How will the website content be organized?
    • The website content will be organized with a clear and intuitive navigation system. The Home page will provide an overview and welcome visitors, showcasing featured content like popular recipes or recent blog posts. The Recipes page will categorize and display cooking ideas, the Videos page will house multimedia tutorials, the Blog page will feature articles and updates, and the Contact page will provide a way for users to reach out or leave feedback.
  2. What sub-topics will the website include?
    • Individual Beer Recipe
    • Video Series on available
  3. What pages will be included on the website?
    • Home
    • Video
    • Contact
    • Blog
  4. Describe the page layout and navigation you are considering.
    • The website layout will feature a clean, user-friendly design with a navigation bar prominently displayed at the top of every page for easy access to Home, Recipes, Videos, Blog, and Contact. The Home page will include a visually appealing banner, highlights of featured content, and quick links to other sections. The Recipes and Videos pages will use grid or list formats for easy browsing, while the Blog will have a chronological or categorized post layout. The **Contact** page will include a form and relevant contact details, ensuring seamless user experience across all pages.
