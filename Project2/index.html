Project 2 - Final Project Proposal

1. Project Concept

Hazy Brewing is a comprehensive website designed for beer enthusiasts and homebrewers, offering a rich array of resources to inspire and guide the brewing process. The site features a detailed recipe library catering to all skill levels, with searchable filters for beer styles, ingredients, and brewing methods. A regularly updated blog delves into brewing techniques, trends, and expert tips while spotlighting the vibrant culture of craft beer. Users can explore step-by-step video tutorials on brewing basics, advanced methods, and equipment care, with live Q&A sessions to connect with professionals. A contact section allows users to submit inquiries, while community-building features, such as forums and social media integration, provide spaces for brewers to share experiences and troubleshoot challenges. With a sleek, user-friendly design optimized for all devices, BrewMaster's Corner aims to be the ultimate hub for brewing knowledge, creativity, and community, elevating the art of homebrewing.
