    Direction on how to brewing beer at home

    To brew beer at home, start by sanitizing all your equipment to prevent contamination.
    Heat water in a large pot and steep crushed malted grains in it to create a mash. Remove the grains,
    then boil the liquid (wort) and add hops for bitterness, flavor, and aroma. Cool the wort quickly and
    transfer it to a sanitized fermenter, adding yeast to start fermentation. Seal the fermenter with an
    airlock and let it sit for 1-2 weeks at a stable temperature. Once fermentation is complete, bottle the
    beer with priming sugar to carbonate it, and let it condition for another 1-2 weeks before enjoying!
